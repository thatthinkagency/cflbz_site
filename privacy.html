Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026. CFLBZ — Central Florida Biz Services (“CFLBZ,” “we,” or “us”).

Information and marketing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for data sales or marketing purposes. We use the information you provide to respond to requests, coordinate scheduling, and connect you with independent contractors and service providers. We do not share your information with third parties for their own marketing unless you have clearly agreed to that specific use, or as required by law.

Booking fees

A booking fee may be required for certain requests. Booking fees are not included in quoted service prices unless explicitly stated in writing. The amount depends on location, type of job, and scope of work, and will be communicated before you commit to a booking where applicable.

Owner, operator, and how work is scheduled

CFLBZ — Central Florida Biz Services is offered by Machine Rythm LLC, a Florida limited liability company registered with the Florida Division of Corporations (Sunbiz). The owner and operator of Machine Rythm LLC seldom performs the work personally; most jobs are fulfilled by trusted independent partners and crews coordinated through this service.

We operate as a hybrid company: the owner may perform work when appropriate, or your project may be outsourced to vetted partners as described elsewhere in this policy. When same-day coverage is required and no partner or crew is available to arrive for your job that day, we may facilitate and assign the crew that will perform the work so your schedule is still addressed as fairly as possible.

Nothing in this section changes the independent-contractor relationship described below; it explains who may show up to perform services and how we route jobs between the owner-operator and partner networks.

Independent contractors, insurance, and liability

All contractors, handymen, and service providers in our network are required to carry their own insurance (and any licenses required for their trade) as applicable. CFLBZ is not responsible for property damage, personal injury, losses, or any other claims arising from work performed by independent pros, and we are not liable for any damages or for matters that could result in legal action between you and a service provider.

By using our service, you understand that you are engaging independent workers, not employees of CFLBZ, and that any disputes regarding workmanship, safety, or payment for labor and materials are primarily between you and the provider, subject to your agreement with them.

What CFLBZ is (and is not)

CFLBZ operates as a booking and networking lead service only. We help administer schedules and introductions between clients and workers. We are not a general contractor for your project unless a separate written agreement says otherwise.

We exist to connect real skilled tradespeople with homeowners and businesses—supporting the community and offering an alternative to impersonal gig-marketplace models that can undervalue licensed, experienced work. Nothing in this policy is intended to replace advice from a qualified attorney or insurance professional.
